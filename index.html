For teachers: how this list is managed
  1. The list lives in a Google Sheet. Tab named Games, with row-1 headers: name, url, tier, grades, category, blurb.
  2. tier is green (always on the board) or bonus (hidden until the Bonus Board is switched on). grades is 3-5, 6-8, or all.
  3. The Bonus Board switch: a second tab named Settings with bonus in cell A1 and on or off in cell B1. Change that one cell to open or close the Bonus Board schoolwide.
  4. One-time setup: in the Sheet, File → Share → Publish to web, then paste the sheet's ID (from its URL, between /d/ and /edit) into the SHEET_ID line at the top of this page's code.
  5. After that: adding a game = adding a row. Hiding a game = changing one cell. No code, ever.
